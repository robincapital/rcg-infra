## Why this exists
Before consolidation, the screener and the per-ticker report had two completely
different price-target engines. The screener's was missing 5 guardrails the
report had, and both were missing 2 critical gates. When a ticker passed the
screener, the report read `target_price` from the screener CSV вҖ” so the report's
better engine never ran on screener-included tickers. INGM published $474.53
target on a $30 stock with RВІ=0.000 on its dominant model, while analyst
consensus said $27.85.

## Three new gates (vs legacy)

### Gate A вҖ” RВІ floor on conviction
Old behavior: a model with RВІ = 0.00 still earned ~50% conviction via
stability and data-points terms.

New behavior:
| RВІ | Conviction |
|----|-----------|
| < 0.20 | **0** вҖ” model is dropped from the blend |
| 0.20 вҖ“ 0.40 | linear ramp (0 вҶ’ full) |
| вүҘ 0.40 | full formula `0.5В·RВІ + 0.3В·stability + 0.2В·n_score` |

Tunable via `PT_R2_FLOOR` and `PT_R2_FULL`.

### Gate B вҖ” Analyst-consensus envelope
Old: when model PT diverged from analyst by >40%, screener flagged with `M*`
but published the model PT unchanged.

New:
| Divergence (% of price) | Source label | Action |
|-------------------------|--------------|--------|
| вүӨ 40% | `Mвң“` | Published unchanged |
| 40 вҖ“ 75% | `M*` | Flagged but published |
| > 75% | `Mвҡ clip` | Clipped to `[analyst Г— 0.5, analyst Г— 1.5]` |

Triggers only when `n_analysts вүҘ 3`. Tunable via `PT_APPLY_ENVELOPE`.

### Gate C вҖ” FCF runaway cap (added Day 2)
The FCF Yield model can produce PTs 5вҖ“10Г— current price when Theil-Sen
extrapolates a strongly-trending FCF series and divides by a small required
yield (~5%). ENVA's FCF model produced PT $1,705 for a $173 stock.

When at least one other valuation model survives, the FCF model PT is
capped at `min(4 Г— last_price, 2 Г— max(other surviving PTs))`. Sets
`applied_cap = "FCF_RUNAWAY_CAP"` and records the uncapped PT for audit.

### Carries over from the report's engine
- Quality-score haircut (0.40вҖ“1.00 multiplier on final blended PT)
- Growth-adjusted EV/Rev anchor (0.6Г— вҖ“ 2.2Г— scalar)
- Mean-reversion cap on low-quality names (`quality < 0.60` вҶ’ blend вүӨ sector anchor)
- 2.5Г— sector ceiling on EV/Rev for healthy names
- Emerging Growth Model 4 (TAM-discounted projection at 25% PV rate)

### Carries over from the screener's engine
- Rate compression on sector multiples (Fed-rate conditional)

## Source labels (`pt_source`)

| Label | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| `M` | Model вҖ” no analyst data available |
| `Mвң“` | Model agrees with analyst (within 40% of price) |
| `M*` | Model flagged but published (40вҖ“75% divergence) |
| `Mвҡ clip` | Model clipped to analyst envelope (>75% divergence) |
| `A` | All models dropped вҖ” falling back to analyst consensus |
| `N/A` | No PT available |
